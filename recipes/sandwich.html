Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Description

Grilled cheese is a classic American sandwich that has been around since 1920. It is a hot sandwich made with buttered and toasted bread and originally filled with American cheese, but is now commonly made with one or more different cheeses.

Ingredients

  • A thick sliced bread
  • ½ tablespoon of butter per slice of bread
  • Cheese, any kind or combination.

Steps

  1. Spread 1/2 Tbsp butter on one side of each slice of bread.

  2. Place a skillet over low to medium/low heat. A griddle should be at 275˚F. Immediately add 2 slices of bread with the butter-side-down.

  3. Stack cheese on one of the pieces of bread, cover with the other piece of toast and flip the sandwich over.

  4. Continue sauteeing, flipping once, until both sides are golden brown and cheese is melted.

  5. Cut the sandwich in half on the diagonal to serve.
